GUIDES & TUTORIALS

How to claim free items on the server?

Make sure you have joined the AquaSphere Roblox group first. After that, log into the game and visit the reward area in the main lobby to claim your special item.

Experiencing lag or stuttering issues?

Lower the graphics quality in your Roblox menu settings to manual (level 1-3). Also, ensure your internet connection is stable to avoid visual data synchronization issues.
